A New Journey

Kunstmuseum Bern

This is the oldest art museum in the country and houses works that date back to the Middle Ages until the present day. Among the 3,000 paintings and sculptures on display are masterpieces by some of the greatest names in international art history. These include Botticelli, Fra Angelico, Monet, van Gogh, and more contemporary artists like Kandinsky, Picasso, and Pollock. Those with knowledge of art will no doubt enjoy embarking on a journey through eight centuries of artistic movements; including impressionism, cubism, expressionism, and surrealism.
